About Us

Wild Circus has been created by James T West and Artemus Gordon. From 1852 to nowadays, the Wild circus has travelled all over the World to offer to children and parents the most spectacular show in the world!!!!

In more than 150 years of traveling, Wild Circus has been able to make you discover extraordinary shows:

  • Nanouk and the flying pinguins have delighted children in the 30's
  • The infamous "Two Arms Jacky" with these man eater tigers. Sadly disappeared in full glory in the love of these animals
  • And now , on stage: the four Trunk: The world best elephant show ever made!!!
